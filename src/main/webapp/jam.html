Jam

Use your locally grown fruit to make yourself some nice jam!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fresh fruit (strawberries, blueberries, etc.)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Fruit: Wash and chop the fruit.
  2. Cook the Mixture: In a large saucepan, combine the fruit, sugar, and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the mixture thickens (about 20-30 minutes).
  3. Jar the Jam: Pour the jam into sterilized jars, seal, and store in a cool place.
