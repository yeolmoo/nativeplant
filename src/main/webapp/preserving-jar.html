Pickled Vegetables

Use your locally grown veggies and make them last even longer!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of fresh vegetables (carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers, or a mix)
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon salt (non-iodized)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • 1 clove garlic (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (e.g., dill, thyme - optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Wash and slice into sticks or rounds.
  2. Sterilize the Jar: Boil the mason jar to sterilize.
  3. Make the Brine: In a saucepan, combine vinegar, water, salt, and sugar. Bring to a boil until dissolved.
  4. Fill the Jar: Add vegetables, garlic, peppercorns, and herbs.
  5. Pour the Brine: Cover the vegetables with hot brine, leaving 1/2 inch of space at the top.
  6. Seal and Cool: Close the jar, cool to room temperature.
  7. Store: Refrigerate and enjoy within 2 months.
