Sandwich

Use freshly harvested vegetables to create a delicious sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Veggies: Slice the tomato and cucumber.
  2. Assemble the Sandwich: Place lettuce on one slice of bread, layer the tomato and cucumber, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Finish: Add cheese if desired, top with the second slice of bread, and enjoy!
